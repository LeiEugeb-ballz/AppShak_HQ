History Features:

Press h to open the History panel
3 Tabs to filter:
Boardroom - only Boardroom discussions
Water Cooler - only water cooler chats
All Events - everything
Click any event to see full conversation details
Back button to return to timeline
Stores up to 500 events for replay
Keyboard Shortcuts:

i - Inbox (approvals)
h - History
u - User location
m - Meeting modal
ESC - Close panels
The history records all events (Boardroom, Water Cooler, Proposals, etc.) so you can go back and review any conversation that happened.

Run the server again with:

uvicorn appshak_office.server:app --host 0.0.0.0 --port 8001
Then access http://localhost:8001 and press h to see the full history.
